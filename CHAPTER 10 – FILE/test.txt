Gojo's Infinity Void: The Ultimate Jujutsu Technique

Introduction
Satoru Gojo's Infinity Void is one of the most powerful and mysterious techniques in Jujutsu Kaisen. It represents the pinnacle of his Six Eyes ability and the Limitless cursed technique, making him virtually unstoppable in combat.

Understanding the Infinity Void
The Infinity Void, also known as the "Unlimited Void," is an extension of Gojo's Limitless technique. While Infinity acts as his passive defense, the Void is an active attack that floods an opponent's consciousness with infinite information. When activated, the target experiences an overwhelming sensation of being trapped in an endless space with no escape.

How It Works
The technique works by overloading the opponent's brain with infinite sensory input. The victim perceives an infinite distance in all directions, making them unable to process or comprehend their surroundings. This mental assault causes the brain to shut down temporarily, leaving the opponent incapacitated and vulnerable.

Power and Limitations
The Infinity Void is extremely powerful but has one critical limitation: it takes time to activate and deploy. During the casting period, Gojo becomes temporarily vulnerable to attacks. Additionally, some opponents with exceptional mental fortitude or specific abilities can resist or mitigate its effects.

Conclusion
Gojo's Infinity Void represents the ultimate expression of his cursed technique, combining offensive and defensive capabilities into one devastating attack. It solidifies his position as the strongest sorcerer in the Jujutsu Kaisen universe and showcases the incredible potential of the Limitless technique.
